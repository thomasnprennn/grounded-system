## Revision vs Scope Change

Revision:

Change within agreed brief. Direction stays the same.

Scope change:

New deliverable, new direction, or change after approval.

Approval at each milestone locks direction.

## Extra Revision

Rp 200.000 / round / element.

Paid before revision work starts.

## Deposit Policy

Deposit is commitment, not booking fee.

- slot is not reserved until deposit is paid
- non-refundable if client cancels after project starts
- transferable if cancelled before kickoff with at least 7 days notice

## Late Payment Protocol

| Timing | Status |
|---|---|
| Day 1–3 | reminder, no penalty |
| Day 4–7 | work pauses |
| After 7 days | project hold, slot may reopen |
| After 14 days | project cancelled, deposit forfeited |

# Approval Policy

## Approval Principle

Approval locks direction.

After approval, changes to direction are scope changes, not revisions.

## Approval Status

- Pending
- Approved
- Revision Requested
- Scope Change
- Locked

## Required Approval Milestones

### Brand System Build

- Strategic Foundation approval
- Visual system approval
- Final brand guide approval

### Full Implementation

- Brand system approval
- Website copy/design approval
- Content calendar approval
- Final delivery approval

# Brand Guide — gr( )unded studio

## 01 — Core Identity

Name: `gr( )unded studio`

Tagline:

> "We make brands look like they know what they're doing."

Mission:

Help indie brands with good products or services but weak visual language look as clear, consistent, credible, and intentional as their actual quality.

Vision:

Become a small selective studio known for a strong point of view and deliberate output, not a high-volume portfolio.

Core belief:

A good product is not enough if customer perception does not catch up with product quality.

## 02 — Core Problem

Many small brands are not failing because the product is bad. They are losing trust because their visual and narrative system is fragmented.

Surface problems:
- inconsistent Instagram feed
- unclear bio
- random color usage
- weak photography direction
- website that does not align with social media
- packaging that feels like a different brand

Root problem:

There is no system.

## 03 — Core Values

### Clarity Over Aesthetics

A good-looking visual is not enough if the brand is still unclear.

### System Thinking

Every output must connect to the bigger system.

### Proof Before Claim

Do not claim credibility. Show it through consistent touchpoints, before/after, customer reaction, and concrete changes.

### Customer Perspective First

Judge every decision from the perspective of someone discovering the brand for the first time.

### Execution Over Perfection

Ship work that is clear, useful, and worthy. Do not use perfectionism as a reason to delay.

## 04 — Personality

- confident without arrogance
- direct without being cold
- curious without being noisy
- selective without being elitist
- intentional without being rigid

Core word: deliberate.

## 05 — Verbal Style

Default language: Indonesian.

Use English terms only when they are more precise:

- clarity
- brand system
- visual language
- brief
- audit
- onboarding
- case study
- intake form

Avoid:
- hype
- hashtags
- emojis
- corporate jargon
- generic claims
- unsupported superlatives

## 06 — Visual World

Keywords:

Warm. Structured. Intentional. Restrained. Confident. Minimal. Moody. Sophisticated. Deliberate. Editorial.

Visual direction:

Warm brutalism — hard materials, warm human presence, editorial restraint, strong shadow, tactile detail.

## 07 — Color System

- Maroon `#643233`
- Deep Maroon `#3D1E1F`
- Dusty Rose `#8C4E50`
- Amber Warm `#C4A882`
- Warm White `#F5F0E8`
- Cream `#E8E0D0`
- Ink `#2C2C2A`

Never use bright, high-saturation colors, random seasonal palettes, or gradients.

## 08 — Typography

- Heading/body/label: DM Sans
- Data/code: DM Mono
- Quote/icon use: Cormorant Garamond Italic

General rule:

Use structure, spacing, and hierarchy before decoration.

Help indie brands with good products or services but weak visual language look as clear, consistent, credible, and intentional as their actual quality.

A small selective studio known for taste, clarity, and deliberate output.

For indie brands that already have good products but do not yet have a visual language that can speak for itself, `gr( )unded studio` builds clarity systems across logo, color, typography, website, Instagram, and customer touchpoints so the brand looks like it knows what it is doing.

The brand is not bad. The system is missing.

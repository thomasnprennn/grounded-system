# Client Status Pipeline

## Status List

### Lead

Potential client identified.

### Contacted

Outbound sent or inbound replied.

### Qualified

Problem and fit are clear enough.

### Proposal Sent

Scope, price, timeline, and next step sent.

### Negotiation

Client is considering or discussing scope/price/timeline.

### Deal Closed

Client confirmed verbally/written, waiting payment or kickoff.

### Active

Project started.

### Waiting Client

Waiting for data, feedback, approval, or payment.

### Revision

Client requested revision within approved scope.

### Approval

Milestone needs client sign-off.

### Completed

Final delivery done.

### Archived

No longer active.

## Rule

Every project must have:

- current status
- next action
- due date
- owner
- blocker if any

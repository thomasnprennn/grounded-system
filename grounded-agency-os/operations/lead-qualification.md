# Lead Qualification

## Fit Signals

Good fit:
- brand already has product/service
- owner feels visual/narrative inconsistency
- brand has ambition to look more credible
- open to diagnosis before design
- understands clarity affects customer trust

Weak fit:
- only wants cheap logo
- wants trend-based design
- refuses to provide context
- expects instant results without system
- asks for speculative work before commitment

## Score

Rate 1–5:

1. Product/service quality signal
2. Clarity problem exists
3. Owner seriousness
4. Budget alignment
5. Timeline realism
6. Taste/vision alignment

Total:

- 24–30: Strong fit
- 17–23: Potential fit
- 10–16: Audit only or nurture
- below 10: No-go

## One Question Rule

Do not ask 5 questions at once.

Ask the one question that reveals the most:

"Kalau harus pilih satu, bagian mana yang paling bikin kamu merasa brand ini belum terlihat sekuat kualitas produknya?"

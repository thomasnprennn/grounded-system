# Sales Outbound Flow

## 01 — Choose Brand

Only contact brands with visible clarity gap and potential alignment.

Look for:
- good product/service signal
- inconsistent visual language
- unclear narrative
- no system across touchpoints
- owner seems active and serious

## 02 — Research First

Before DM, note:

- one specific observation
- why it matters
- what package might fit
- whether you have enough evidence

## 03 — Opener DM

Structure:

1. Specific observation
2. Why it matters
3. One soft question

Example:

"Satu observasi kecil: bio IG-nya belum terlalu menjawab untuk siapa brand ini — orang yang baru menemukan mungkin perlu scroll lebih dalam sebelum ngerti.

Aku bantu brand visual dan sistem komunikasi untuk indie brand. Ini disengaja atau belum sempat dirapikan?"

## 04 — Follow-up

Follow-up 1: 5–7 days.
Follow-up 2: 10–14 days.
Then stop.

## 05 — Stop Conditions

Stop when:
- no response after second follow-up
- clear no
- no fit
- only asking for free advice without progress

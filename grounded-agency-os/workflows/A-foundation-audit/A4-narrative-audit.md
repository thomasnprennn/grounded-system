# A4 — Narrative Audit

## Purpose

Audit how clearly the brand communicates who it is, who it serves, and why people should care.

## Inputs Needed

- Instagram bio
- 5–15 captions
- website copy if available
- product descriptions
- any tagline or brand statement

## Files to Read

- AGENTS.md
- brand/tone-of-voice.md
- clients/[client]/01-intake.md

## Output to Create

- bio clarity score
- voice consistency notes
- value proposition clarity
- caption pattern
- narrative gaps

## Save To

`/clients/[client]/foundation-audit/04-narrative-audit.md`

## Human Review

Human validates tone interpretation and whether examples are accurate.

## Quality Gate

- uses actual copy provided
- no invented captions
- identifies shifts in tone
- clear impact on customer decision

## AI Instruction

```txt
Analyze the brand narrative from provided copy. Score bio clarity, voice consistency, value proposition clarity, and message coherence. Identify the top narrative gaps and explain how they affect customer confidence.
```

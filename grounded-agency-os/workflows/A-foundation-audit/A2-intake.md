# A2 — Intake

## Purpose

Collect enough information to audit the brand without guessing.

## Inputs Needed

- brand context
- current visual assets
- screenshots
- captions/bio
- customer comments/DM patterns
- website/packaging/store touchpoints if any

## Files to Read

- AGENTS.md
- brand/brand-guide.md
- templates/intake-form-template.md

## Output to Create

- custom intake form
- internal intake summary
- list of missing evidence

## Save To

`/clients/[client]/01-intake.md and /clients/[client]/foundation-audit/02-intake-summary.md`

## Human Review

Human checks whether the available data is enough for audit.

## Quality Gate

- questions are specific
- no unnecessary long form
- every question helps diagnosis
- missing evidence is clear

## AI Instruction

```txt
Create a Foundation Audit intake form and summarize the client context. Separate confirmed facts from assumptions. List what evidence is still needed before visual/narrative/customer perception audit.
```

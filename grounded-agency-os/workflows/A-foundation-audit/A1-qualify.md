# A1 — Qualify

## Purpose

Decide whether the lead is a fit for Foundation Audit, Brand System Build, or not worth pursuing.

## Inputs Needed

- brand name
- Instagram handle or website
- business type
- current problem in their own words
- timeline
- budget signal if available

## Files to Read

- AGENTS.md
- brand/offer-positioning.md
- operations/lead-qualification.md

## Output to Create

- fit score
- likely pain points
- recommended package
- qualifying questions
- Go / No-go decision

## Save To

`/clients/[client]/00-client-profile.md and /clients/[client]/foundation-audit/01-qualify.md`

## Human Review

Human decides whether to accept, reject, or ask one more question.

## Quality Gate

- one clear package recommendation
- no over-questioning
- clear missing information
- does not promise results

## AI Instruction

```txt
Evaluate this lead for gr( )unded studio. Diagnose whether their issue is a clarity/system problem. Recommend one next step: Foundation Audit, Brand System Build, Full Implementation, or No-go. Ask only the single most important follow-up question if data is insufficient.
```

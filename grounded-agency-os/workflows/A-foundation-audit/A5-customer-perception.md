# A5 — Customer Perception Audit

## Purpose

Understand what customers likely feel, ask, or misunderstand based on provided comments, DM patterns, testimonials, or owner notes.

## Inputs Needed

- comments
- DM questions
- customer objections
- reviews
- sales/inquiry notes

## Files to Read

- AGENTS.md
- clients/[client]/01-intake.md
- workflows/A-foundation-audit/A3-visual-audit.md
- workflows/A-foundation-audit/A4-narrative-audit.md

## Output to Create

- perception gaps
- common confusion points
- trust blockers
- decision friction points

## Save To

`/clients/[client]/foundation-audit/05-customer-perception.md`

## Human Review

Human checks if interpretation is fair and not overclaimed.

## Quality Gate

- marks low-data assumptions
- explains customer hesitation
- ties perception to evidence
- no fake sentiment analysis without data

## AI Instruction

```txt
Analyze customer perception from provided data. Identify what people do not understand, what creates hesitation, and what questions keep repeating. If customer data is missing, create a provisional perception map and mark it clearly as assumption-based.
```

# F — Content System Overview

This workflow helps create consistent content for `gr( )unded studio` or a client.

Content must not be random posts. It should build a world and prove a point of view.

## Content Pillar Logic

A strong content system should include:

1. Point of View — what the studio believes.
2. Diagnosis — problems brands keep missing.
3. Proof — before/after, case study, process insight.
4. Taste World — visual atmosphere and editorial references.
5. Offer Education — how the service works and who it is for.

## Rule

No generic educational carousel. Every post must carry a specific diagnosis or point of view.

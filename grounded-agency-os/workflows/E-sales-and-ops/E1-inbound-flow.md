# E1 — Inbound Flow

## Purpose

Handle people who come first. Start with one question: what phase is the brand in and what problem do they most want to solve? Then diagnose whether the issue is unclear problem, system gap, or implementation need.

## Output

Save relevant messages, decisions, and next steps inside:

`/clients/[client]/messages/`
`/clients/[client]/approvals/`
`/clients/[client]/03-decisions.md`

## Quality Gate

- one clear next step
- no pressure language
- no vague pricing
- package matched to problem
- client decision documented

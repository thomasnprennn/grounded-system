# E2 — Outbound Flow

## Purpose

Choose brands worth contacting. Research before DM. Open with one specific observation. Follow up after 5–7 days, then again after 10–14 days. Stop after no response or clear no.

## Output

Save relevant messages, decisions, and next steps inside:

`/clients/[client]/messages/`
`/clients/[client]/approvals/`
`/clients/[client]/03-decisions.md`

## Quality Gate

- one clear next step
- no pressure language
- no vague pricing
- package matched to problem
- client decision documented

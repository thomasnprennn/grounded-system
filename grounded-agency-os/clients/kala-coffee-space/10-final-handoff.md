## 04 - Final Root Problems

These are the current root problems from the diagnosis report draft. They still require human review before client-facing final delivery.

1. Peran utama KALA belum terkunci.

Customer tercatat bingung apakah KALA adalah serious coffee shop, coworking space, casual hangout spot, atau hybrid yang disengaja. The system issue is not a surface-level design taste issue; KALA needs one clear first meaning for customers.

2. Evidence belum cukup untuk menentukan penyebab visual atau narrative.

The problem is recorded, but the source material is incomplete. Screenshot notes, actual screenshots, captions, bio text, raw comments, website/menu/touchpoints, and positioning details are still missing or empty.

3. Touchpoint belum dipetakan ke satu makna yang sama.

The current materials do not yet show whether Instagram, captions, menu, space, website, signage, and customer messages all point to the same brand meaning.
